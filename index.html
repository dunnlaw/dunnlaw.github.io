OUR PHILOSOPHY


Our goal is to provide quality legal service on a cost-effective basis to self-insureds and insurance carriers. We believe that in keeping our firm small, we are better able to achieve results of a higher quality. In fact, we believe that the quality of the service we provide rivals, if not exceeds, that produced at the offices of our much larger competitors. We recognize that claims and litigation detract from our clients' ability to conduct their business, and we strive to make the process as painless for our clients as possible. By maintaining an organized, informative, and personal attorney/client relationship, we work to serve our clients by delivering effective, and productive, legal representation.
